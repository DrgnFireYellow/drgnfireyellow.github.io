In this tutorial I will show you how to create an animated terminal welcome screen.

First you need to install the needed packages.

sudo apt install figlet
sudo apt install lolcat

The next step is to open either your .bashrc or your .zshrc file.

nano ~/.bashrc
or
nano ~/.zshrc

At the top of one of those files enter the following replacing USERNAME with your username and os with your operating sytstem.

figlet -c Welcome, USERNAME | lolcat -a -d 1
figlet -f term -c OS: os | lolcat
